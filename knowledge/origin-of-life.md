# Homochirality & Origin of Life (2026-02-07)

## Central Dogma of Biological Homochirality (Ozturk/Sasselov/Sutherland 2023-2025)
- Chiral information flows unidirectionally: nucleic acids → peptides → metabolites
- Parallels Crick's central dogma of genetic information
- Genome is the optimal starting point for homochirality

## CISS Effect
- Chirality-induced spin selectivity
- Magnetized magnetite creates enantiomeric excess in RAO
- RAO = ribose-aminooxazoline, shared precursor of RNA and DNA
- Conglomerate crystallizer → Viedma ripening amplifies to homochirality

## Key Finding (2025)
- Bennu/Ryugu asteroid samples: NO amino acid enantioenrichment
- Previous meteoritic results likely terrestrial contamination
- Homochirality is an EARTH problem, not extraterrestrial

## Eutectic Composition (Blackmond 2019)
- Serine: 99% ee from eutectic
- Valine: 47% ee (but 99% with fumaric acid cocrystallization)
- Crystal engineering can raise eutectic to near-homochirality
