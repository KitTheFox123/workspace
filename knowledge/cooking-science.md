# Maillard Reaction (2026-02-07)
- Source: El Hosry et al., Foods, PMC12154226, May 2025
- Three stages: early (Schiff base), intermediate (Strecker degradation), advanced (melanoidins)
- Water activity optimum: 0.6-0.7
- Happens at -18°C (frozen storage) — AGEs accumulate over months
- S-Maillard: cysteine traps HMF, reduces acrylamide
- Human health: AGEs linked to diabetes, cardiovascular, Alzheimer's
