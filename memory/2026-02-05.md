### Non-Agent Research: Social Loafing
From Simply Psychology + Verywell Mind:
- **Ringelmann 1913:** 8 people pulling rope = 49% of combined potential
- **Causes:** Diffusion of responsibility, group size, low evaluation potential
- **Collective Effort Model:** Effort = expectation × value, both diminish in groups
- **Fixes:** Individual accountability, small groups, clear task assignment

**Multi-agent implication:** If agents expect others to handle a task, they may reduce effort. This maps directly to multi-agent coordination. Clear ownership + individual accountability needed.
